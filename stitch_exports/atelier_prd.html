# The Editorial Direction ## Product Overview **The Pitch:** An ultra-premium digital portfolio positioning Yusmel Colombo not as a laborer, but as an artisanal craftsman of electricity. By leveraging high-fashion editorial layouts, we elevate the perception of his technical expertise to that of a luxury service provider. **For:** High-end residential clients, architects, and luxury contractors in Luxembourg who value precision, aesthetics, and multilingual professionalism. **Device:** Desktop (Primary focus for architectural context) **Design Direction:** "Architectural Digest meets Technical Precision." Massive whitespace, confident typography, and zero container borders create a fluid, continuous narrative flow rather than discrete sections. **Inspired by:** *Kinfolk Magazine* layouts, *Aesop* product pages, *The New York Times Magazine* feature spreads. --- ## Screens - **The Cover (Home):** Immersive, cinematic introduction establishing tone and identity. - **The Narrative (About):** A narrow, typeset biography column akin to a magazine article. - **The Craft (Expertise):** Asymmetrical grid showcasing skills with numerical typographic drama. - **The Chronology (Experience):** A linear, refined timeline of professional evolution. - **The Ledger (Details):** Structured data view of languages, education, and soft skills. - **The Connection (Contact):** A dark, sophisticated footer acting as the final call to action. --- ## Key Flows **Flow: The Discovery** (Prospective Client vetting Yusmel) 1. User lands on **The Cover** -> Sees full-screen cinematic imagery with "COLOMBO" superimposed in massive, thin type. 2. User scrolls down to **The Narrative** -> Reads a refined, centered bio surrounded by breathing room. 3. User continues to **The Craft** -> Scans "01 Electricité" and "02 IOT" treated as headlines. 4. User reaches **The Connection** -> Copies the phone number from the dark footer block. ---
Design System ## Color Palette - **Primary:** `#1A1A1A` - Primary text, headings (Charcoal) - **Background:** `#FFFFFF` - Main canvas (Pure White) - **Surface:** `#F9FAFB` - Subtle alternating sections (Ghost White) - **Text:** `#404040` - Body copy (Dark Grey) - **Muted:** `#9CA3AF` - Dates, metadata - **Accent:** `#3B82F6` - Selected states, subtle underlines (Soft Electric Blue) ## Typography **Philosophy:** A tension between ultra-modern sans-serifs and a high-contrast editorial serif. - **Display (Hero):** *Playfair Display*, 300 Italic, 160px. (Elegant, massive) - **Headings (Section):** *Playfair Display*, 700, 48px. (Bold, authoritative) - **Numerals:** *Bodoni Moda*, 900, 80px. (High contrast, sculptural) - **Body:** *Switzer* (or Inter tight), 300, 18px. (Clean, technical, legible) - **Captions:** *Switzer*, 500, 12px, Uppercase, tracking 2px. **Style Notes:** - **Zero Borders:** Separation is created solely through spacing (160px+ margins) and font weight contrast. - **Asymmetry:** Content rarely spans full width unless it is an image. Text hugs a central 600px column or adheres to a 12-column grid edge. - **Images:** Sharp corners, no shadows. Treat images like architectural blocks. ## Design Tokens ```css :root { --color-primary: #1A1A1A; --color-bg: #FFFFFF; --color-surface: #F9FAFB; --color-accent: #3B82F6; --font-serif: 'Playfair Display', serif; --font-numeral: 'Bodoni Moda', serif; --font-sans: 'Switzer', sans-serif; --spacing-section: 180px; --spacing-element: 32px; } ```

Screen Specifications ### 1. The Cover (Hero) **Purpose:** Instant emotional impact. Establishes Yusmel as a premium brand. **Layout:** Full viewport height (`100vh`). Image is absolute, covering the screen. **Key Elements:** - **Background Image:** High-res photo of electrical conduit work or architectural lighting, desaturated or high-key. - **Overlay Text:** "YUSMEL COLOMBO" - Font: *Playfair Display*, 120px, White (mix-blend-mode: exclusion or difference if needed). - Position: Absolute center, spanning 90% width. - **Subtitle:** "Électricien & Spécialiste IOT — Luxembourg" - Font: *Switzer*, 14px, Uppercase, tracking wide. - Position: Bottom left, 48px from bottom. - **Scroll Indicator:** A single vertical line (1px wide, 60px tall) animating downwards. **States:** - **Load:** Text fades in `ease-out` over 1.2s. Image slightly scales down from 1.1 to 1.0. **Responsive:** - **Mobile:** Text breaks to two lines ("YUSMEL" / "COLOMBO"). Font size 64px. --- ### 2. The Narrative (Profile) **Purpose:** Humanizes the technician. **Layout:** Single narrow column (max-width: 600px) centered in a sea of white space. **Key Elements:** - **Section Label:** "01 — Profil" (Sticky on left edge of viewport on desktop). - **Portrait:** Floating image to the right of the text column (parallax effect). - Size: 300x400px. - **Body Copy:** "From precise metrology to complex HVAC installations, my journey is defined by technical rigor. Based in Leudelange, I bring a multilingual, artisanal approach to modern electrical systems." - Style: *Switzer* 300, 20px, leading 1.6. - **Key Stats Row:** Beneath text, 3 columns. - "DAP Certified" | "6 Languages" | "17 Years Tech Exp" --- ### 3. The Craft (Expertise) **Purpose:** Showcase technical range with editorial flair. **Layout:** 2x2 Grid, but staggered (zigzag). **Key Elements:** - **Skill Cards:** No background, just text groupings. - **Numerals:** "01", "02", "03", "04" - Style: *Bodoni Moda*, 80px, Color: `#E5E7EB` (Light Gray) to `#1A1A1A` on hover. - **Headlines:** - 1. Électricité Générale - 2. Métrologie & Précision - 3. Informatique & IOT - 4. Climatisation - **Description:** 3 lines of copy per skill, *Switzer* 16px. **Interactions:** - **Hover:** The Numeral fills with charcoal color. A thin blue line (`#3B82F6`) grows under the headline. --- ### 4. The Chronology (Experience) **Purpose:** Demonstrates stability and growth without a boring table. **Layout:** A vertical line runs down the center. Nodes branch left and right alternatively. **Key Elements:** - **Timeline Axis:** 1px solid `#E5E5E5`. - **Entries:** - **Left Side:** Date Range (e.g., "Oct 2023 — Présent") - Style: *Switzer*, 14px, Muted. - **Right Side:** Role & Company (e.g., "DAP en électricité — Global Facilities") - Style: *Playfair Display*, 24px, Regular. - **Data Points:** - 2023: Global Facilities - 2022: MySafety - 2019: Ouvrier polyvalent - 2017: Co-gérant restaurant - 2014: Aide-installateur HVAC - 2006: Métrologie **Responsive:** - **Mobile:** Line moves to far left. All content stacks on the right. --- ### 5. The Ledger (Education & Languages) **Purpose:** Hard data presented elegantly. **Layout:** Split screen. Left 50% = Languages. Right 50% = Education. Background: `#F9FAFB`. **Key Elements:** - **Language List:** - Format: Language Name (Bold) .......... Level (Right aligned) - Visual: Dotted leader line between name and level. - "Français — B1.2", "Espagnol — Maternelle", "Luxembourgeois — B1". - **Education Stack:** - Simple list, no bullets. - "2022 — DAP Electricien" - "2020 — JR Web Developer" - "2003 — Métrologie" --- ### 6. The Connection (Footer) **Purpose:** High-contrast closure. Direct contact. **Layout:** Full width block. Dark Charcoal background (`#1A1A1A`). **Key Elements:** - **Layout:** Three distinct columns. - **Col 1 (Availability):** "Currently accepting new projects in Leudelange & Greater Luxembourg." - Text: White, 14px. - **Col 2 (Coordinates):** - "17a, rue du Lavoir L-3358" - "+352 621 430 283" - "yucolaguilar@gmail.com" - Style: White, 18px, underlined on hover. - **Col 3 (Signature):** "Yusmel Colombo © 2024" **Interactions:** - **Email Click:** Copies to clipboard. Toast notification appears: "Address Copied."

Build Guide **Stack:** HTML + Tailwind CSS v3 **Build Order:** 1. **Global Typography & Grid:** Define `font-family` classes and the master spacing variables. The "Editorial" feel lives or dies by the typography implementation. 2. **The Cover (Hero):** Build the full-screen landing. Ensure text scales correctly on resize without breaking the layout. 3. **The Connection (Footer):** Establish the bookends of the site. 4. **The Narrative (Text Column):** Implement the narrow reading experience. 5. **The Craft (Grid):** Implement the staggered grid layout. 6. **The Chronology:** Complex flexbox/grid implementation for the timeline. **Tailwind Config Nuances:** - Extend `spacing` to include massive values like `128` (32rem) for margins. - Add custom `fontFamily` for Playfair Display and Switzer. - Set default `container` center and padding to `0` (we will handle margins manually for the "borderless" feel).
